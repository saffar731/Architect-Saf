Tips & Tricks

Precision Drawing: Use the command line to enter exact coordinates. Type 100,50 for absolute coordinates or @50,30 for relative.
Object Snap: Press F3 to toggle OSNAP. Hover near endpoints, midpoints, centers, and intersections for precise snapping.
Ortho Mode: Press F8 to constrain drawing to horizontal/vertical lines. Perfect for architectural drawings.
Layers: Organize your drawing by assigning different elements to layers. Toggle visibility and lock layers to prevent accidental edits.
Undo/Redo: Use Ctrl+Z and Ctrl+Y to navigate through your edit history. The system maintains up to 50 steps.
Keyboard Shortcuts: Press ? anytime to see the full list of keyboard shortcuts.
